About Us

We are a company devoted to not only transforming your body, but also to prepare you for competitive races. The next time you enter a 5k, marathon, or any other type of cardio event, you will be prepared to set personal records and gain an upper hand on your competition. Our program is available for people of all different backgrounds and levels. Whether you are a beginner runner, who is trying to get back in shape, or an Olympic athlete who is training for the next triathalon, we guarantee our program will help support your goals.

Beginning Runners and Casuals

Are you someone who likes to run for fun? Do you need to shed some pounds before you attend an important event? Perhaps you want to run to supplement weight lifting. Whatever your goal is, our program serves these purposes. By subscribing to MRP, you have access to quality workout videos that are sure to burn fat as well as build stronger muscles. We also provide a meal guide that allows you to eat the correct foods based off your goal.

Competitive Runners

Our program also helps those who aspire to win their next race. For competitive athletes, we supply more intensive workouts, an online fitness log that tracks tempo and race paces, and a personalized calorie and food tracker based off your physique, race pace, and goal pace. Competitive runners also have access to online personal trainers, who will guide them through their fitness plan and meal plan.
